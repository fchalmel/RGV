Compare signatures

The TOXsIgN compare module, is a feature allowing users to compare toxicogenomics signatures between them or in several custom lists. This module is adapted from P. Bardou et al.. It works and displays intersection between up to 6 signatures in a Venn diagram. In order to compare signatures across species, only the HomoloGene Ids associated to the signature are used and displayed.
